A nice man in an airport told me about Wizz Air's All You Can Fly which lets you fill seats on flights within 3 days of their departure for 10EUR a flight. I like spontianuity so I went for it.

# Jan 3rd : Liverpool
Flight from Belfast to LPL

# Jan 4th - 8th : Warsaw, Poland
Flight from LPL to Warsaw

Staying in the center of Warsaw at a hostel for $20 p/Night

# Jan 8th - Mon 12th : Larnaca, Cyprus
I stayed on a boat during my time here. It was (mostly) peaceful and enjoyable, but there were a couple of mosquitos which were fuckers.

# Mon 12th Jan - Fri 16th Jan : Chisinau, Moldova
$48 p/Night avg between 2 nice AirBnBs

# Fri 16th Jan - 21st : Athens, Greece
I love Athens very much, it is a beautiful city full of life.
